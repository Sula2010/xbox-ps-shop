About Us

Xbox is a video gaming brand that consists of five home consoles It also includes applications, Xbox Cloud Gaming, and online services These online services are the Xbox network and Xbox Game Pass. The brand is produced by Microsoft Gaming, a division of Microsoft.

The PlayStation 5 (PS5) is a home video game console developed by Sony. It was announced as the successor to the PlayStation 4 in April 2019. It launched on November 12, 2020, in Australia, Japan, and North America. It was released worldwide a week later, alongside the Xbox Series X/S. The PS5 is part of the ninth generation of video game consoles.
